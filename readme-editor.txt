Level editor for AEROMIC

CAMERA CONTROLS:
Right click + drag to move camera
- + for zoom
Home key to reset camera

BRUSH CONTROLS:
Left click to draw a brush
Left click on an already existing brush to select it, hold left click and drag to move brush.
Left click on the resize node of a selected brush and drag to resize a brush.

CONTROL PANEL:
Textbox is the levels raw data. You can edit it and then press the [Enter] button to enter the raw data in.
[Save level] to save level to a file.
[Load level] to load level from a file.
[Launch in-game] load the level in-game. Save your level to file beforehand.

IMPORTANT NOTE: Dont put spaces in level name and brush keys
