AEROMIC


HOW TO PLAY:


WSAD keys       to move

SPACE           to jump

MOUSE           to aim

LEFT CLICK      to fire

UP/DOWN ARROW   change music

F5              fullscreen

M               mute music



WINDOWS:

AEROMIC.exe



LINUX:

you need these packages:

libsdl2-2.0-0

libsdl2-dev

libsdl2-mixer-2.0-0

libsdl2-mixer-dev

libsdl2-image-2.0-0

libsdl2-image-dev

libsdl2-ttf-2.0-0

libsdl2-ttf-dev


then do

$ make

and

$ ./AEROMIC
